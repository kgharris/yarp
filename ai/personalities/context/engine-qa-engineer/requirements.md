# Engine QA Engineer — Requirements Phase

## Focus

- Are computational rules specified unambiguously, or are there hidden assumptions?
- Are invariants documented as requirements (not just implied)?
- Are numerical precision and rounding expectations stated?
- Are there concrete examples with expected outputs to verify correctness of understanding?
