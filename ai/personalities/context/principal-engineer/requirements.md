# Principal Engineer — Requirements Phase

## Focus

- Are requirements complete and internally consistent (no conflicts, no circular dependencies)?
- Are dependencies between requirements explicit (what must be true before what can happen)?
- Is there enough specificity to design from, or are requirements too vague?
- Is the scope appropriate (not too ambitious, not too narrow) and technically feasible?
